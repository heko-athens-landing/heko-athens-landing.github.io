About

Where history
meets modernity

Heko Collection Athens 2 is a design studio apartment in Pangrati — one of Athens' most authentic and relaxed neighbourhoods. The Panathenaic Stadium is an 8-minute walk away, and the city's key attractions are easily reachable on foot or by metro.

Every room is a thoughtfully designed space where ancient motifs weave with contemporary comfort. Everything here is crafted to feel like home — a very beautiful one.
